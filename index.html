About Project 120 Africa

Project 120 Africa is a high-impact youth empowerment initiative focused on equipping the next generation with essential tech, media, and business skills. We empower 120 individuals per state with comprehensive training in six distinct skill sets to thrive in the digital landscape.

For this year, we will visit four states. Each state will host a four-week registration, followed by a two-day conference, one month of internship, and a certification ceremony.

Early bird registration is ₦25,000 (limited time offer) vs. the standard fee of ₦30,000.
