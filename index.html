Project 120 Africa

Project 120 Africa is a high-impact youth empowerment initiative focused on equipping the next generation with essential tech, media, and business skills. The project empowers 120 individuals per state through comprehensive training in six distinct skill sets, ensuring personalized learning. For this year, we are visiting four states with an intensive registration period, followed by a two-day conference, one month of internship, and a certification ceremony.

Early bird registration is available at ₦25,000, with the standard fee set at ₦30,000.
