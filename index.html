About Project 120 Africa

Project 120 Africa is a high-impact youth empowerment initiative focused on equipping the next generation with essential tech, media, and business skills. This project empowers 120 individuals per state, providing them with the necessary tools and knowledge to thrive in the rapidly evolving digital landscape.

For this year, we will be visiting four states, each with a comprehensive curriculum aimed at enhancing both technical, business, and media-related competencies. The project is broken down into six distinct skill sets, each comprising 20 participants to ensure effective and personalized teaching.

Early bird registration costs ₦25,000, with a standard fee of ₦30,000. These funds go toward covering the cost of workshops, materials, and operational expenses for the program.

Project Structure:
- Registration (4 weeks per state)
- 2-Day Conference for all registered participants
- 1-Month Internship Program
- Certification Ceremony with awards for outstanding participants

Skill Sets Offered:
Data Analysis, Email Marketing, Digital Creatives, Video Editing, Content Writing, Social Media Management, Digital Marketing, Funnel Marketing, Google Ads, YouTube Optimization & Monetization, Virtual Assistance, and more.
